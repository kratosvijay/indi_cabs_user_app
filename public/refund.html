Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Cancellations

Users may cancel a ride request at any time prior to the arrival of the driver at the pickup location. A cancellation fee may apply if:

  • The driver has already reached the pickup location.
  • The driver has already spent a significant amount of time/distance traveling towards the pickup point.

2. Refunds

Refunds are processed for the following cases:

  • Technical errors resulting in double charging.
  • Rides that were canceled by the driver but charged to the user.
  • Overcharging due to incorrect fare calculation by the app.

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the ride completion or cancellation.

3. Refund Process

Once approved, the refund will be credited back to your original payment source (Bank Account, Credit/Debit Card, Wallet) within 5-7 business days.

4. Pricing Currency

All transactions and refunds are processed in Indian Rupees (INR).
